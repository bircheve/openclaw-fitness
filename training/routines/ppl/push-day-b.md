# Push Day B - Shoulder Focus

Shoulder-dominant push day with secondary chest and tricep work.

## Session Flow

1. **Warmup** (10-15 min)
2. **Lifting** (~75 min): Execute exercises in order
3. **Cardio Finisher** (35 min): Stairmaster at moderate intensity

## Exercise Order

| # | Exercise | Sets | Reps | Rest | Notes |
|---|----------|------|------|------|-------|
| 1 | Seated DB Press | 4 | 8-10 | 60s | Primary shoulder movement |
| 2 | Incline Dumbbell Press | 3 | 10-12 | 60s | Upper chest maintenance |
| 3 | Lateral Raise | 4 | 12-15 | 45s | Side delt emphasis |
| 4 | Rear Delt Fly | 3 | 15-20 | 45s | Rear delt isolation |
| 5 | Cable Fly | 3 | 12-15 | 45s | Chest pump |
| 6 | Tricep Pushdown | 3 | 12-15 | 45s | Tricep isolation |
| 7 | Close Grip Bench | 3 | 10-12 | 60s | Tricep compound |

## Execution Notes

- Shoulder press is the priority - put most energy here
- All movements use 3-5 second eccentrics
- Last set of each exercise to failure
- Lateral raises can be higher volume/lighter weight

## Total Volume

- Shoulders: 11 sets
- Chest: 6 sets
- Triceps: 6 sets

## Substitutions

If equipment unavailable:
- Seated DB Press → Machine Shoulder Press
- Rear Delt Fly → Face Pulls
- Close Grip Bench → Dips

## Shoulder Health Notes

- Warm up thoroughly before pressing
- If shoulder pain, reduce weight or substitute
- Face pulls/rear delt work is important for balance
- Don't press behind the neck

## Intra-Workout

Sip throughout lifting:
- 25g whey protein
- 10g BCAAs
- 5g glutamine

## Post-Workout

Within 10 minutes of cardio completion:
- 40g whey protein
- 32oz electrolyte drink
