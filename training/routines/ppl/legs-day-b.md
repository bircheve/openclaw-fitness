# Legs Day B - Hamstring/Glute Focus

Hip-hinge dominant leg day with emphasis on posterior chain.

## Session Flow

1. **Warmup** (10-15 min)
2. **Lifting** (~75 min): Execute exercises in order
3. **Cardio Finisher** (30 min): Stairmaster at moderate intensity

## Exercise Order

| # | Exercise | Sets | Reps | Rest | Notes |
|---|----------|------|------|------|-------|
| 1 | Romanian Deadlift | 4 | 8-10 | 90s | Primary hamstring |
| 2 | Leg Curl | 4 | 10-12 | 60s | Hamstring isolation |
| 3 | Barbell Squat | 3 | 10-12 | 90s | Quad maintenance |
| 4 | Walking Lunge | 3 | 12/leg | 60s | Unilateral + glutes |
| 5 | Hip Thrust | 3 | 12-15 | 60s | Glute isolation |
| 6 | Leg Extension | 3 | 12-15 | 45s | Quad pump |
| 7 | Calf Raise | 4 | 15-20 | 45s | Standing or seated |

## Execution Notes

- RDL is the priority - feel the hamstring stretch
- All movements use 3-5 second eccentrics
- Hip thrust: squeeze glutes hard at top, hold 1 sec
- Last set of each exercise to failure

## Total Volume

- Hamstrings: 8 sets
- Glutes: 6 sets (plus glute work in other exercises)
- Quads: 6 sets
- Calves: 4 sets

## Hamstring Focus

To feel hamstrings in RDL:
- Push hips back, not down
- Keep slight knee bend, don't bend more
- Feel the stretch at the bottom
- Straps are encouraged to not limit weight

## Substitutions

If equipment unavailable:
- RDL → Stiff Leg Deadlift or Good Morning
- Walking Lunge → Bulgarian Split Squat
- Hip Thrust → Glute Bridge or Cable Pull Through

## Cardio Note

Cardio is 30 min (not 35) on leg days because legs are fatigued. Still mandatory.

## Intra-Workout

Sip throughout lifting:
- 25g whey protein
- 10g BCAAs
- 5g glutamine

## Post-Workout

Within 10 minutes of cardio completion:
- 40g whey protein
- 32oz electrolyte drink
