# Push Day A - Chest Focus

Primary chest development day with emphasis on upper chest through incline pressing.

## Session Flow

1. **Warmup** (10-15 min)
2. **Lifting** (~75 min): Execute exercises in order
3. **Cardio Finisher** (35 min): Stairmaster at moderate intensity

## Exercise Order

| # | Exercise | Sets | Reps | Rest | Notes |
|---|----------|------|------|------|-------|
| 1 | Incline Barbell Press | 4 | 8-10 | 60s | Primary chest movement |
| 2 | Flat Dumbbell Press | 4 | 10-12 | 60s | Mid-chest focus |
| 3 | Cable Fly | 3 | 12-15 | 45s | Drop set on last set |
| 4 | Seated DB Press | 3 | 10-12 | 60s | Shoulder compound |
| 5 | Lateral Raise | 4 | 12-15 | 45s | Side delt isolation |
| 6 | Tricep Pushdown | 3 | 12-15 | 45s | Tricep isolation |
| 7 | Overhead Tricep Extension | 3 | 12-15 | 45s | Drop set on last set |

## Execution Notes

- All movements use 3-5 second eccentrics
- Focus on mind-muscle connection over weight
- Last set of each exercise to failure
- Double progression: hit top of rep range on all sets before increasing weight

## Total Volume

- Chest: 11 sets
- Shoulders: 7 sets
- Triceps: 6 sets

## Substitutions

If equipment unavailable:
- Incline Barbell Press → Incline Smith Machine
- Flat DB Press → Flat Barbell Press
- Cable Fly → Pec Deck Machine
- Overhead Extension → Skull Crushers

## Intra-Workout

Sip throughout lifting:
- 25g whey protein
- 10g BCAAs
- 5g glutamine

## Post-Workout

Within 10 minutes of cardio completion:
- 40g whey protein
- 32oz electrolyte drink
