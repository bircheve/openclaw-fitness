# Legs Day A - Quad Focus

Squat-dominant leg day with emphasis on quad development.

## Session Flow

1. **Warmup** (10-15 min)
2. **Lifting** (~75 min): Execute exercises in order
3. **Cardio Finisher** (30 min): Stairmaster at moderate intensity

## Exercise Order

| # | Exercise | Sets | Reps | Rest | Notes |
|---|----------|------|------|------|-------|
| 1 | Barbell Squat | 4 | 8-10 | 90s | Primary compound |
| 2 | Leg Press | 4 | 10-12 | 60s | Quad focus |
| 3 | Romanian Deadlift | 3 | 10-12 | 60s | Hamstring work |
| 4 | Leg Extension | 3 | 12-15 | 45s | Drop set on last |
| 5 | Leg Curl | 3 | 12-15 | 45s | Hamstring isolation |
| 6 | Calf Raise | 4 | 15-20 | 45s | Standing or seated |

## Execution Notes

- Squats are the priority - full depth, controlled tempo
- All movements use 3-5 second eccentrics
- Leg press: feet mid-platform for quad focus
- Last set of each exercise to failure

## Total Volume

- Quads: 11 sets
- Hamstrings: 6 sets
- Calves: 4 sets

## Squat Depth

At minimum, hit parallel (hip crease at knee level).

If mobility allows, go deeper:
- Better glute activation
- More quad stretch
- But not at the expense of form

## Substitutions

If equipment unavailable:
- Barbell Squat → Hack Squat or Smith Squat
- Leg Press → Hack Squat
- RDL → Lying Leg Curl (add volume)

## Cardio Note

Cardio is 30 min (not 35) on leg days because legs are fatigued. Still mandatory.

## Intra-Workout

Sip throughout lifting:
- 25g whey protein
- 10g BCAAs
- 5g glutamine

## Post-Workout

Within 10 minutes of cardio completion:
- 40g whey protein
- 32oz electrolyte drink
