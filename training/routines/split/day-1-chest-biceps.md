# Day 1 — Chest + Biceps

## Target Muscles
- **Primary**: Chest (all heads)
- **Secondary**: Biceps

## Block Structure

### Phase 2 — Primary Compound
- 1 flat press variation (barbell or dumbbell)
- 1 incline press variation (barbell or dumbbell)
- Sets: 3-4 each, reps: 8-12, rest: 90-120 sec

### Phase 3 — Secondary Hypertrophy
- 1 chest fly or crossover variation
- 1 chest isolation (cable, pec deck, or machine)
- 1 barbell or dumbbell curl
- 1 hammer or concentration curl
- Sets: 3 each, reps: 12-15, rest: 45-75 sec

### Phase 4 — Finisher
- 1 cable or bodyweight burnout for chest (push-ups, cable crossovers)
- High reps: 15-20+

### Phase 5 — Cardio
- 30-40 min stairmaster, moderate intensity
