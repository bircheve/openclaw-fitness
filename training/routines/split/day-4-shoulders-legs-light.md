# Day 4 — Shoulders + Legs (Light)

## Target Muscles
- **Primary**: Shoulders (all heads)
- **Secondary**: Quads (light), Rear Delts

## Block Structure

### Phase 2 — Primary Compound
- 1 overhead press variation (barbell, dumbbell, or machine)
- 1 moderate leg press or front squat (lighter than Day 2 — maintenance volume)
- Sets: 3-4 each, reps: 8-12, rest: 90-120 sec

### Phase 3 — Secondary Hypertrophy
- 1 lateral raise variation (dumbbell, cable, or machine)
- 1 face pull or rear delt fly
- 1 front raise or upright row
- 1 leg extension (light, 2-3 sets)
- Sets: 3 each, reps: 12-15, rest: 45-75 sec

### Phase 4 — Finisher
- 1 shoulder burnout (cable lateral raise drop set, band pull-aparts)
- High reps: 15-20+

### Phase 5 — Cardio
- 20-30 min stairmaster (shorter session — legs were worked)
