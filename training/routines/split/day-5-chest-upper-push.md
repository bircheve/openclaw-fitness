# Day 5 — Chest + Upper Push

## Target Muscles
- **Primary**: Chest (emphasis on angles not hit on Day 1)
- **Secondary**: Triceps, Front Delts

## Block Structure

### Phase 2 — Primary Compound
- 1 incline press variation (swap angle/equipment from Day 1)
- 1 dip variation (weighted dips, machine dips)
- Sets: 3-4 each, reps: 8-12, rest: 90-120 sec

### Phase 3 — Secondary Hypertrophy
- 1 cable fly or machine fly (different angle from Day 1)
- 1 front raise or chest press machine
- 1 tricep isolation (pushdown or overhead extension)
- Sets: 3 each, reps: 12-15, rest: 45-75 sec

### Phase 4 — Finisher
- 1 push burnout (push-ups, cable crossover drop set)
- High reps: 15-20+

### Phase 5 — Cardio
- 30-40 min stairmaster, moderate intensity
