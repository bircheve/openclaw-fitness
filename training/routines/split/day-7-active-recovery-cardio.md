# Day 7 — Active Recovery Cardio

No lifting. This is a dedicated cardio session for active recovery.

## Target
- **Primary**: Cardiovascular conditioning
- **Secondary**: Active recovery, fat oxidation

## Warm-up (5 min)
- 5 min dynamic stretching — hip circles, leg swings, thoracic rotations, shoulder dislocates
- Light foam rolling if available (quads, lats, glutes)

## Cardio (75 min)

**Primary**: StairMaster at moderate, steady-state intensity

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Duration | 75 min continuous |
| Intensity | Zone 2 — moderate, conversational pace |
| Heart Rate | 120-140 BPM |
| Goal | Active recovery, not performance |

**Acceptable substitutions** (same duration and intensity):
- Incline treadmill walk (10-15% grade, 3.0-3.5 mph)
- Rowing machine (steady state, 18-22 strokes/min)
- Mix of the above (e.g., 40 min stairmaster + 35 min incline walk)

## Intensity Guidelines

This is **active recovery**, not a HIIT session:
- Keep heart rate in the 120-140 BPM range throughout
- You should be able to hold a conversation the entire time
- If you're breathing hard or sweating heavily, dial it back
- The goal is blood flow, not calorie burn or conditioning gains
- Don't push through fatigue — this session sets up the next training week
