# Day 2 — Legs + Side Delts

## Target Muscles
- **Primary**: Quads, Hamstrings, Glutes
- **Secondary**: Side Delts, Calves

## Block Structure

### Phase 2 — Primary Compound
- 1 squat variation (back squat, front squat, hack squat)
- 1 leg press or lunge variation
- 1 hip hinge (Romanian deadlift or stiff-leg deadlift)
- Sets: 3-4 each, reps: 8-12, rest: 90-120 sec

### Phase 3 — Secondary Hypertrophy
- 1 leg extension
- 1 leg curl
- 1 lateral raise variation (dumbbell, cable, or machine)
- 1 calf raise variation
- Sets: 3 each, reps: 12-15, rest: 45-75 sec

### Phase 4 — Finisher
- 1 leg burnout (walking lunges, leg press drop set, or bodyweight squats)
- High reps: 15-20+

### Phase 5 — Cardio
- **None** — never cardio after leg day
