# Day 3 — Back + Triceps

## Target Muscles
- **Primary**: Back (lats, upper back, rear delts)
- **Secondary**: Triceps

## Block Structure

### Phase 2 — Primary Compound
- 1 row variation (barbell row, dumbbell row, or T-bar row)
- 1 vertical pull (pull-ups, lat pulldown)
- Sets: 3-4 each, reps: 8-12, rest: 90-120 sec

### Phase 3 — Secondary Hypertrophy
- 1 cable row or machine row
- 1 rear delt fly variation
- 1 tricep pushdown variation (rope, bar, or V-bar)
- 1 overhead tricep extension (cable or dumbbell)
- Sets: 3 each, reps: 12-15, rest: 45-75 sec

### Phase 4 — Finisher
- 1 back or tricep burnout (straight-arm pulldown, band pull-aparts, dips)
- High reps: 15-20+

### Phase 5 — Cardio
- 30-40 min stairmaster, moderate intensity
