# Cable Fly

Chest isolation movement with constant tension throughout the range of motion.

## Why This Exercise

- Constant tension (unlike dumbbells which lose tension at top)
- Excellent for mind-muscle connection
- Great finishing exercise for chest pump
- Easy to do drop sets (just move the pin)

## Pulley Height Guide

| Pulley Position | Emphasis | Motion |
|-----------------|----------|--------|
| High | Lower chest | High-to-low arc |
| Shoulder height | Mid chest | Straight across |
| Low | Upper chest | Low-to-high arc |

## Drop Set Protocol

Excellent for finishing chest:
1. Working weight x 12-15 to failure
2. Drop 20-30%, immediately rep to failure
3. Drop again, rep to failure
4. Total: 25-35 reps across the drop set

## Technique Focus

This is an isolation exercise - leave your ego at the door.

- Light to moderate weight only
- Perfect form trumps heavy weight
- You should feel your chest working, not your shoulders or arms
- If you can't squeeze at the center, weight is too heavy

## Variations

- High cable fly (high-to-low)
- Low cable fly (low-to-high)
- Single arm cable fly
- Pec deck machine (similar movement pattern)
