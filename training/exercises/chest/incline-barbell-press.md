# Incline Barbell Press

Primary upper chest developer and foundation compound movement for push days.

## Why This Exercise

- Best exercise for upper chest development
- Builds overall pressing strength
- Transfers well to overhead pressing
- Allows for progressive overload with small weight jumps

## Bench Angle Guide

| Angle | Emphasis |
|-------|----------|
| 15-20 degrees | Low incline, more mid-chest |
| 30 degrees | Standard incline, balanced upper/mid |
| 45 degrees | High incline, maximum upper chest |
| 60+ degrees | Too high, becomes shoulder press |

## Warmup Protocol

Before working sets:
1. Bar only x 15
2. 50% working weight x 12
3. 70% working weight x 8
4. 85% working weight x 4 (optional)

## Variations

- [Incline Dumbbell Press](incline-dumbbell-press.md) - greater ROM, unilateral work
- Incline Smith Machine Press - stable, good for failure training
- Low Incline Press - more mid-chest involvement
